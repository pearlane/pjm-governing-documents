4. Jurisdiction. 
 
The Parties agree that (i) any dispute or conflict requesting the relief in Operating Agreement, 
Schedule 10, section 3.1 and Operating Agreement, Schedule 10, section 3.2(a) shall be 
submitted to FERC for hearing and resolution; (ii) any dispute or conflict requesting the relief in 
Operating Agreement, Schedule 10, section 3.2(c) may be submitted to FERC or any court of 
competent jurisdiction for hearing and resolution; and (iii) jurisdiction over all other actions and 
requested relief shall lie in any court of competent jurisdiction.
