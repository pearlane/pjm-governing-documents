6.2 Return of Capital Contributions.   
 
(a) In the event Members are required to contribute capital to the LLC in accordance with 
Operating Agreement, section 5.1, the LLC shall request the Transmission Owners to recover 
such working capital through charges under the Tariff as provided in Operating Agreement, 
Schedule 3.  In the event all or a portion of the working capital is recovered pursuant to the 
Tariff, such amount(s) shall be returned to the Members in accordance with their actual 
contributions. 
 
(b) Except for return of capital contributions and liquidating distributions as provided in the 
foregoing section and Operating Agreement, section 6.3, respectively, the LLC does not intend 
to make any distributions of cash or other assets to its Members.
