15.4 Obligations of a Member or Participant in Default. 
 
A Member or Participant found to be in default shall take all possible measures to mitigate the 
continued impact of the default on the Members not in default, including, but not limited to, 
loading its own generation to supply its own load to the maximum extent possible.
