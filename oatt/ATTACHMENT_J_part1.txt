provide a specific LMP Benefit.  Where a Required Transmission Enhancement subject to this 
section (b)(vi) consists of a single transmission element or multiple transmission elements that

transmission elements or portions of the transmission elements located in such Zone.  Merchant 
Transmission Facilities shall not be assigned cost responsibility for a Required Transmission 
Enhancement subject to this section (b)(vi).
