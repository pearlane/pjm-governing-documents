ATTACHMENT H-29 
 
Annual Transmission Rates – Transource Pennsylvania, LLC 
 
1. Transource Pennsylvania, LLC’s (“Transource Pennsylvania”) annual transmission 
revenue requirement (“ATRR”) is equal to the results of the formula and its associated 
attachments, as shown in Attachment H-29A posted on the PJM Internet site, which 
reflect Transource Pennsylvania’s facilities within PJM.  The ATRR determined pursuant 
to Attachment H-29A shall be implemented pursuant to the Formula Rate 
Implementation Protocols set forth in Attachment H-29B. 
 
2. The ATRR posted in (1) shall be effective until amended by Transource Pennsylvania or 
modified by the Commission.
