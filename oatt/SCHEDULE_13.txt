SCHEDULE 13 
EXPANSION COST RECOVERY CHARGE (ECRC) 
 
(a) In accordance with the Settlement reached in Docket No. EL05-74-000, Transmission 
Customers using Point-to-Point and Network Integration Transmission Service to serve load 
within a PJM Zone other than the Dominion Zone shall pay an Expansion Cost Recovery Charge 
(ECRC), as defined below.  The ECRC rates are designed to recover revenues to compensate the 
AEP East Operating Companies, Commonwealth Edison Company, and The Dayton Power and 
Light Company (collectively the “Companies”) for non-capital expenses incurred by PJM and 
funded by the Company, including carrying charges through the end of the ten-year cost 
recovery period.  PJM shall distribute all revenues collected under this schedule each calendar 
month to the Companies in proportion to the allocation percentages in part (e) below. 
 
(b) Two separate ECRC rates will be assessed each year.  One ECRC rate will apply 
throughout the New PJM Zones, and a different rate will apply in the Duquesne Zone and the 
Pre-Expansion PJM Zones.  PJM shall apply the ECRC rates specified in this schedule for 2005 
and 2006, and shall calculate new ECRC rates on an annual basis, effective January 1 of each 
year, in the manner specified herein for 2007 and each superseding year of the cost recovery 
period.  PJM shall calculate and post revised ECRC rates on the PJM website by December 15 
each year, but may adjust such rates and posting prior to issuing bills for the following January to 
reflect updates in the prior year’s zonal peak load (“Network Service Peak Loads”). 
 
(c) The total ECRC revenue to be collected over the ten-year period beginning May 1, 2005 
(“Cost Recovery Period”) is $52,549,556.  The portion of that amount to be collected in the New 
PJM Zones is $30,864,989.  The amount to be collected in the Duquesne Zone and the Pre-
Expansion PJM Zones is $21,684,567.  The ECRC rates will be designed to collect 
approximately 1/10 of the total amounts each twelve months of the Cost Recovery Period when 
applied to the Network Service Peak Loads of Network Customers in each of the applicable 
Zones.  The charges provided for under this Schedule 13 shall terminate effective on the day on 
which the sum total of the revenues collected hereunder equals $52,549,556, with $30,864,989 
having been collected in the New PJM Zones and $21,684,567 having been collected in the 
Duquesne and Pre-Expansion Zones. 
 
 (d) As illustrated in Table 1 below, the Formula for ECRC rates that PJM shall calculate each 
year through 2014 is as follows: 
 
ECRC = Collection Amount / NSPL / # months in period. 
 
In 2015, unless collections have been completed earlier, PJM shall design the ECRC rate in the 
Duquesne Zone and the Pre-Expansion Zones to collect, during the first four months of 2015, the 
remaining portion of the $21,434,899 that has not been collected through the end of 2014 in 
these Zones.  Likewise, unless collections have been completed earlier, in 2015 PJM shall design 
the ECRC rate in the New PJM Zones to collect, during the first four months of 2015, the 
remaining portion of the $30,509,603 that has not been collected through the end of 2014.  Table 
1 shows the target amounts of ECRC revenue to be collected during each year of the Cost 
Recovery Period, and illustrates the calculation of the ECRC rates for 2005 and 2006.
