ATTACHMENT H-31 
 
Annual Transmission Revenue Requirement – Ohio Valley Electric Corporation 
For Network Integration Transmission Service 
1. The Annual Transmission Revenue Requirement for purposes of the Network Integration 
Transmission Service is $11,256,927, and the rate for Network Integration Transmission 
Service is $5,163.73 per megawatt per year, which reflect the cost of providing 
transmission service over the 345 kV transmission facilities of the Ohio Valley Electric 
Corporation (“OVEC”).   
2. The rate in (1) shall be effective until amended by OVEC or modified by the 
Commission. 
3. In addition to the rate set forth in (1), the Network Customer purchasing Network 
Integration Transmission Service shall pay for any transmission congestion charges, in 
accordance with the provisions of the Tariff, and any amounts necessary to reimburse the 
Transmission Owners for any amounts payable by them as sales, excise, “Btu,” carbon, 
value-added or similar taxes (other than taxes based upon or measured by net income) 
with respect to the amounts payable pursuant to the Tariff.
