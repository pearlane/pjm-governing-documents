conditions of Transmission Service, Network Integration Transmission Service, and other 
transactions with entities under the Tariff, set forth throughout the Tariff, shall constitute rates, 
terms, and conditions of PJMSettlement service.

subject of transactions it undertakes with PJMSettlement as a buyer.  In accordance with and

products that are the subject of transactions it undertakes with each buyer.  The warranties set 
forth in this paragraph are provided only in connection with the requirements established by the 
FERC for PJMSettlement to serve as a Counterparty.  Accordingly, any enforcement of, or 
challenge to, the warranties set forth in this paragraph shall be heard exclusively before the

under the Uniform Commercial Code or common law that might be enforceable in federal or 
state courts or in any forum other than FERC.

to other non-transmission transactions that are (1) bilateral transactions between market 
participants reported to the Transmission Provider, and (2) self-supplied or self-scheduled 
transactions reported to the Transmission Provider.

consent of the other Party, which consent shall not be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or 
delayed.  Any such assignment or delegation made without such written consent shall be null and

assignor’s cost estimates for the Project; and (ii) the assignee is eligible to be a Designated Entity

obligations hereunder absent the written consent of the other Party.    In no circumstance, shall

Agreement diminish the rights of the Transmission Provider under this Agreement, the Tariff, or 
the Operating Agreement.  Any assignees that will construct, maintain, or operate the Project

entitle the receiving Party to allege a cause of action for anticipatory Breach of this Agreement.
