Section VII. Competitive Bid Concessions 
 
For transmission development projects assigned to PECO as a result of the PJM competitive 
project sponsor process, PECO may, in its sole discretion, agree with PJM to apply a 
Competitive Bid Concession that will result in a lower net revenue requirement on a project-
specific basis than that which would otherwise be produced by the PECO Formula Rate. Any 
Competitive Bid Concession will appear as a zero or negative input to the Formula Rate, and 
will be determined on a project-specific basis using a workpaper that will be provided to 
Interested Parties, in native format with all cells and formulae intact, as supporting 
documentation for each PECO Annual Update.
