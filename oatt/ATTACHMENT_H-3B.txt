ATTACHMENT H-3B 
 
Load Power Factor Charge Applicable 
to Service to the Interconnection Points 
Between the Delmarva Power & Light Company System and the 
Distribution Systems owned by the Delaware Cities and Towns of Smyrna, Seaford, 
Middletown, New Castle, Newark, Milford and Clayton 
 
In the event that any one of the distribution systems (the “Municipalities”) located at the 
Interconnection Points identified in this Attachment H-3B is, under its Mutual Operating 
Agreement or other similar agreement with Delmarva Power & Light Company, considered to be 
non-compliant with respect to its load power factor requirements (“Load Power Factor 
Requirements”), a Load Power Factor Charge shall be assessed to the Municipality or, if 
applicable, to its Load Serving Entity (“LSE”).  Said Load Power Factor Charge shall be based 
upon the following: 
 
1. No Load Power Factor Charge shall be assessed until Delmarva seeks PJM 
approval of specific facilities (the “Facilities”) to be installed on the Delmarva 
transmission system for the purpose of bringing the Municipality into compliance 
with its Load Power Factor Requirements. 
 
2. The Load Power Factor Charge will commence in the billing month following the 
in-service date of the Facilities.   
 
3. The Load Factor Power Charge will be based upon a direct allocation of the cost 
of the Facilities to the Municipality or, if the Facilities provide for more load 
factor correction than required to bring the Municipality into compliance, upon a 
share of the cost of such Facilities that is equal to the portion of the load factor 
correction used to bring the Municipality into compliance. 
 
4. To the extent that the cost of the Facilities are recovered from the Municipality or 
its LSE through the Load Factor Power Charge, said cost shall not be recovered 
from the Municipality or its LSE in any other manner.   
 
5. The monthly Load Power Factor Charge shall be derived as follows: 
 
LPFC = AC * AS * ACC/100 * 1/12 
 
Where: 
 
LPFC  =  Monthly Load Power Factor Charge  
AC      =  The net book value of the Facilities (as determined on the 
first day of each calendar year during which the LPFC is 
assessed).

 AS       = The Municipality’s Assigned Share of the Facilities which shall be equal 
to the ratio of (i) the MVars required to bring the Municipality into compliance and (ii) 
the MVar capacity of the Facilities 
ACC   = Transmission level Annual Carrying Charge rate equal to 
20.82%. 
 
For a period of six months following completion of the required Facilities, 
the Municipality or its designee shall be permitted to audit the actual costs 
incurred by Delmarva that are used for AC.  Any dispute with respect to 
such costs shall be resolved through the PJM dispute resolution procedure. 
 
6. The Load Factor Power Charge shall be assessed to the Municipality or, if applicable, its 
LSE, for a total of 480 months.
