ATTACHMENT H-3A 
 
Loss Factors Applicable to Service Metered at the Interconnection Points 
Between the Delmarva Power & Light Company Systems and the 
Distribution Systems owned by the Delaware Cities and Towns of Smyrna, Seaford, 
Middletown, New Castle, Newark, Milford and Clayton 
 
Metered values for service to the Interconnection Points identified in this Attachment H-3A shall 
be adjusted for transmission or system losses (as applicable) by application of a loss factor.  For 
the Hourly Energy Obligation, the applicable loss factor is equal to 1.02861.  For the annual 
Capacity and Transmission Peak Load Contribution calculations, the applicable loss factor is 
equal to 1.03193.  Application of the loss factor provided for herein shall not, in any event, result 
in metered values for service to the Interconnection Points identified in this Attachment H-3A 
being adjusted for transmission losses more than one time.  The loss factor provided herein shall 
not be used, and shall be replaced by generally applicable loss factors set forth in the PJM Tariff, 
if and when PJM includes any such loss factors in its Tariff.
