ATTACHMENT H-3C 
 
Under-Frequency Load Shedding Charge Applicable 
to Service to the Interconnection Points 
Between the Delmarva Power & Light Company System and the 
Distribution Systems owned by the Delaware Cities and Towns of Smyrna, Seaford, 
Middletown, New Castle, Newark, Milford and Clayton 
 
In the event that any one of the distribution systems (the “Municipalities”) located at the 
Interconnection Points identified in this Attachment H-3C is not in compliance with the EDC 
under-frequency load shedding requirements of PJM, the Municipality’s Mutual Operating 
Agreement, or other similar agreement with Delmarva Power & Light Company (“Under-
frequency Load Shedding Requirements”), an Under-frequency Load Shedding Charge shall be 
assessed to said Municipality or, if applicable, to its Load Serving Entity (“LSE”).  The Under-
frequency Load Shedding Charge in any month shall be equal to any penalty or charge assessed 
by PJM to Delmarva in said month that is attributable to said Municipality’s failure to meet the 
Under-frequency Load Shedding Requirements.  Any PJM assessed penalty or charge recovered 
by Delmarva under this Attachment H-3C shall not be recovered by Delmarva from the 
Municipality or its LSE in any other manner.
