2) Revenue Distribution 
 
 a) Notwithstanding the provisions of this Paragraph 2, PJM shall implement revenue 
distribution in accordance with the provisions of the March 29, 2007 PJM Transmission Owners 
Agreement – Administrative Committee Clarification of SECA Revenue Distribution Resolution 
(“Resolution”) for any settlement agreement accepted or approved by the Commission in Docket 
Nos. ER05-6-001, et al. where one or more Transmission Owner is a party to such settlement 
agreement.  Similarly, PJM shall distribute revenues collected after the end of the SECA 
transition period for SECA charges that were previously billed but not collected by PJM or the 
Midwest ISO in accordance with the Resolution and accompanying revenue distribution matrix.  
The Resolution and accompanying revenue distribution matrix are posted on the PJM website.  
Except where modified by the Resolution or the terms of a Commission order, PJM shall be 
responsible for distributing revenues to the PJM Transmission Owners in accordance with Table 
3 below.

 b) Revenues distributed to the PJM East Transmission Owners and Allegheny Power 
under this Attachment R shall be allocated among those Transmission Owners in accordance 
with Section 5.4 of the PJM Transmission Owners Agreement and the PJM West Transmission 
Owners Agreement. 
 
 c) For purposes of this Attachment, a "PJM Existing Transaction" shall mean 
transmission service to the Border of PJM under Schedules 7 and 8, and Attachment H-A of the 
Tariff, serving load located within MISO during the period that this Attachment is in effect, 
where the reservation request was made prior to November 17, 2003 or service commenced prior 
to April 1, 2004.  If MISO informs PJM that it has reduced the SECA payments to recover PJM 
Transmission Owners' lost revenues to reflect a PJM Existing Transaction, PJM shall treat the 
amounts received under that PJM Existing Transaction in the same manner as SECA revenues 
received from MISO, and shall allocate the revenues in accordance with the percentages in Table 
3 of paragraph 2 a) of this Attachment R.
