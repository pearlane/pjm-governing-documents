ATTACHMENT G 
 
Network Operating Agreement 
 
The Operating Agreement of the PJM Interconnection, L.L.C., as amended from time to time, 
shall constitute the Network Operating Agreement for Network Customers serving load in the 
PJM Region.  For other Network Customers, a Network Operating Agreement shall be 
negotiated, which shall address:  (a) Control Area requirements, (b) redispatch procedures, (c) 
metering, (d) Control Area and data equipment, (e) operating requirements, (f) operating 
information requirements, (g) network planning, (h) the character of the service to be provided, 
(i) the transfer of power and energy through other systems, and (j) such other matters as the 
parties deem appropriate.
