ATTACHMENT H-18 
 
Annual Transmission Rates – Trans-Allegheny Interstate Line Company 
 
1. The annual transmission revenue requirement is equal to the results of the formula and its 
associated attachments shown in Attachment H-18A posted on the PJM Internet site 
(“Formula Rate”) which reflects the facilities of Trans-Allegheny Interstate Line 
Company (“TrAILCo”) within the Allegheny Power Zone, Pennsylvania Electric 
Company Zone, Metropolitan Edison Company Zone, Jersey Central Power and Light 
Company Zone, and American Transmission Systems, Incorporated Zone.  The rate 
determined pursuant to Attachment H-18A shall be implemented pursuant to the Formula 
Rate Implementation Protocols set forth in Attachment H-18B.  Service utilizing other 
facilities will be provided at rates determined on a case-by-case basis.  
 
2. The revenue requirement in (1) shall be effective until amended by TrAILCo or modified 
by the Commission.
