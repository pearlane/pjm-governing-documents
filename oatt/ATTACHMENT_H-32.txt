ATTACHMENT H-32 
Annual Transmission Revenue Requirements and Rates - AMP Transmission, LLC  
1. The annual revenue requirement and rates for integrated transmission facilities located 
within PJM and owned or leased by AMP Transmission, LLC (“AMPT”) shall be equal to 
the results of the formula set forth in Attachment H-32A applied in accordance with the 
Formula Rate Implementation Protocols set forth in Attachment H-32B. 
2. If the costs of AMPT facilities located within PJM are to be recovered through the PJM 
Tariff from one or more specific PJM Transmission Zones through charges to Network 
Customers purchasing Network Integration Transmission Service, AMPT will identify each 
such Transmission Zone and the revenue requirement to be recovered from, and rate to be 
charged by PJM in, such Zone by modifying Attachment H-32C to add such specification 
using a sequentially numbered suffix for each such Zone.
