ATTACHMENT H-6A 
 
Other Supporting Facilities Charges -- FirstEnergy Pennsylvania Electric Company 
(Penelec Zone) 
 
As provided in Attachment H-6, Paragraph 1, service utilizing facilities at voltages below 
46 kV to serve certain Pennsylvania Municipal Utilities and Cooperatives will be provided at the 
rates set forth below (“Other Supporting Facilities Charges” or “OSFCs”). 
 
Berlin, Pennsylvania    0.175¢ per kWh 
 
East Conemaugh, Pennsylvania  0.113¢ per kWh 
 
Girard, Pennsylvania    0.057¢ per kWh 
 
Hooversville, Pennsylvania   0.213¢ per kWh 
 
Smethport, Pennsylvania   0.147¢ per kWh 
 
Summerhill, Pennsylvania   0.283¢ per kWh 
 
Allegheny Electric Cooperative, Inc.  $2.66/kW/month* 
 
Allegheny Electric Cooperative, Inc.  $8.72/kW/month** 
 
 
* The rate of $2.66/kW/month shall apply to all Allegheny Electric Cooperative, Inc. delivery 
points for delivery by FirstEnergy Pennsylvania Electric Company located in the Penelec Zone at 
23 kV or 34.5 kV and shall be phased in over time in accordance with an applicable service 
agreement entitled an “Interconnection Agreement for Wholesale Load” (“Service Agreement”) 
between the Parties on file with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission.  The OSFC in the 
Service Agreement is also referred to as a “Monthly Distribution Charge.” 
 
** The rate of $8.72/kW/month shall apply to all Allegheny Electric Cooperative, Inc. delivery 
points for delivery by FirstEnergy Pennsylvania Electric Company located in the Penelec Zone at 
12.5 kV and shall be phased in over time in accordance with an applicable service agreement 
entitled an “Interconnection Agreement for Wholesale Load” (“Service Agreement”) between 
the Parties on file with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission.  The OSFC in the Service 
Agreement is also referred to as a “Monthly Distribution Charge.”

Monthly Distribution Charges ($/kW) 
The Monthly Distribution Charge (“MDC”) of $2.66/kW/month shall be phased in over time in accordance with the 
following table, which assumes an effective date of October 1, 2022 for the Service Agreement: 
  Calendar Year/Quarter % of MDC MDC 
2022 Q4 55% $1.46/kW/month 
2023 Calendar Year 60% $1.59/kW/month 
2024 Calendar Year 60% $1.59/kW/month 
2025 Calendar Year 60% $1.59/kW/month 
2026 and thereafter 100% $2.66/kW/month 
 
The MDC of $8.72/kW/month shall be phased in over time in accordance with the following table, which assumes an 
effective date of October 1, 2022 for the Service Agreement: 
Calendar Year/Quarter % of MDC MDC 
2022 Q4 55% $4.79/kW/month 
2023 Calendar Year 60% $5.23/kW/month 
2024 Calendar Year 60% $5.23/kW/month 
2025 Calendar Year 60% $5.23/kW/month 
2026 and thereafter 100% $8.72/kW/month 
   
 
The above tables shall be adjusted by mutual agreement of the Parties if the effective date of the Service Agreement is 
established by FERC at a date subsequent to October 1, 2022 in order to give effect to the Parties’ intent to have the 
phase-ins of the MDCs over the course of three (3) years with 100% of the MDCs to apply no later than the fourth (4th) 
anniversary of the effective date of the Service Agreement.  Notwithstanding section 13.1 of the Service Agreement, the 
Parties agree not to exercise rights afforded them by Sections 205 and 206 of the Federal Power Act to alter the MDCs 
during the ten-year period beginning with the effective date of the Service Agreement as established by FERC.
