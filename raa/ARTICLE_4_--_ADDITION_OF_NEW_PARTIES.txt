ARTICLE 4 -- ADDITION OF NEW PARTIES 
 
 Each Party agrees that any entity that (i) is or will become a Load Serving Entity, (ii) 
complies with the process and data requirements set forth in RAA, Schedule 1, and (iii) meets 
the standards for interconnection set forth in RAA, Schedule 2 shall become a Party to this 
Agreement and shall be listed on RAA, Schedule 17 upon becoming a party to the Operating 
Agreement, and execution of a counterpart of this Agreement.
