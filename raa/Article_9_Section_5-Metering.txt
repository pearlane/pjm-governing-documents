9.5 Metering. 
 
 Each Party shall comply with the metering standards for the PJM Region, as set forth in 
the PJM Manuals, as well as any further metering requirements applicable to Price Responsive 
Demand, where such is relied upon for an adjustment to peak load pursuant to RAA, Schedule 
6.1.
