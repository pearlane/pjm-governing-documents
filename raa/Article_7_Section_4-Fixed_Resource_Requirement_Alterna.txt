7.4 Fixed Resource Requirement Alternative. 
 
 A Party that is eligible for the Fixed Resource Requirement Alternative may satisfy its 
obligations hereunder to provide Unforced Capacity by submitting and adhering to an FRR 
Capacity Plan and meeting all other terms and conditions of such alternative, as set forth in this 
Agreement.
